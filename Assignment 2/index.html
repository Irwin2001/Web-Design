A Short Summary

A Man Out Of Time

Alric was once a hero, but now lives in the distant future. He's a man out of time, in a era that no longer needs savoirs. He still holds onto the values of hope, faith, and compassion in a society that grown numb and sterile. He is haunted by his own grief of his family, friends, and lost time. Alric navigates a disconnected world, struggling to find meaning. This is a story about living between what was and what is, about being good when goodness feel forgotten, and about a quiet man trying to heal in a world that moved on without him.
